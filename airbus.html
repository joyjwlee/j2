Airbus

Advanced automation with modern avionics. Electric fly-by-wire flight control system for improved safety and performance.

Boeing

One of the first aircraft manufacturers. Based in the US, manufacturing modern, efficient, and iconic aircraft.

Cessna

Most common General Aviation aircraft manufacturer. Specializing in piston, turboprop, and even small jets. Suitable for private pilots or PPL students in training.

Embraer

Famous and well known for its regional jets. With its short to medium haul range, it is great for the regional aviation infrastructure.

McDonnell Douglas

Iconic classic airliners. Most famous for its very successful MADDOG MD80 and the classic tail-dragger airliner DC3.

Bombardier

Large variety of short to medium haul jets and turboprops. Most well known for its Dash 8 series, as well as its C-series adapted by Airbus and CRJ business jets.
